Semiconductor graph

This graph shows the production of semiconductors, and we can see that from the start of 2020 up until the middle
of May, production decreased and stagnated due to COVID. At the same time people were stuck at home during
the lockdowns, many of whom had to work from home. We can assume that the demand
for technology went up since most people had to adjust to working from home and staying home all the time.
Though production went up, the prices still kept going up, and based on the principles
of supply and demand we can assume that the increase in production from the middle of May did not keep up with the demand.
